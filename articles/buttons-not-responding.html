Buttons Not Responding

If some or all buttons on your remote are not responding, the issue may be physical or software-related.

Restart Your Roku

Unplug your Roku device for 10 seconds, then plug it back in.

Clean the Remote

Dirt or debris under the buttons can prevent them from working. Wipe the remote with a dry cloth and tap it gently to dislodge debris.

Check for Stuck Buttons

Press each button firmly. A stuck button can prevent other buttons from registering.

Re-Pair the Remote

Hold the pairing button for 5 seconds to reconnect the remote to your Roku device.
