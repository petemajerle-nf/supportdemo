Remote Has No Power

Your remote may appear dead or unresponsive due to a power issue.

Replace Batteries

Swap out the batteries with a fresh set. Mixing old and new batteries can prevent the remote from powering on.

For Rechargeable Remotes

Plug the remote into a USB charger. A blinking status light means it is charging. Allow up to 2 hours.

Inspect Battery Terminals

Look for corrosion or debris on the battery contacts. Clean gently with a dry cotton swab.
