Remote Won’t Pair With Roku Device

Wireless Roku remotes must pair before they can be used. Here’s how to fix pairing problems.

Use the Pairing Button

Open the battery compartment and hold the pairing button for 5 seconds.

Move Closer to the Device

Pairing works best when the remote is within 10 feet of the Roku player.

Restart Both Remote and Device

Power cycle the Roku device and remove/reinsert the batteries before pairing again.

Check Wi-Fi Environment

Wireless remotes pair over your local Wi-Fi environment. Interference from routers, walls, or other devices may interrupt pairing.
