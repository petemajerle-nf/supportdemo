Intermittent Remote Connection

Your remote may connect and disconnect repeatedly. Try the steps below.

Check Battery Strength

Weak batteries can cause the remote to lose connection temporarily. Replace batteries with new ones.

Reduce Wireless Interference

Move routers, microwaves, or Bluetooth devices away from your Roku device to minimize interference.

Re-Pair the Remote

Hold the pairing button for 5 seconds to re-establish a stable connection.

Restart the Roku Player

Unplug power for 10 seconds and reconnect. This resets the remote connection service.
